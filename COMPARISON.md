## Evaluation Dimensions

When comparing products in this category, focus on:

- privacy model
- local storage vs cloud dependency
- support for multiple model providers
- latency and responsiveness
- meeting history and retrieval
- screenshot and context capture
- update reliability
- packaging quality on macOS and Windows
- transparency of licensing and source availability
